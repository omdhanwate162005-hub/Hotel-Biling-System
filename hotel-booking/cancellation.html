📋 Our Policies

Cancellation Policy

We understand plans can change. Here's everything you need to know about cancellations, modifications, and refunds.

Last updated: April 14, 2026

✅

Free Cancellation

Cancel up to 48 hours before check-in for a full refund — no questions asked.

⚠️

Late Cancellation

Cancellations within 24–48 hours of check-in incur a one-night charge.

❌

No-Show

Failure to check in without notice results in full booking amount being charged.

01 Standard Cancellation Terms

Cancellation Window Charge Refund
More than 48 hours before check-in No Charge 100% Refund
24 – 48 hours before check-in 1 Night Charge Remaining Refunded
Less than 24 hours before check-in 50% Charge 50% Refund
No-Show (no cancellation) Full Charge No Refund

02 Refund Process

1

Submit Cancellation Request

Cancel your booking via email, phone, or the booking confirmation page. You'll receive an acknowledgment within 1 hour.

2

Processing Period

Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days of the cancellation request being approved.

3

Credit to Account

The refund will be credited to your original payment method. Bank processing may take an additional 5–7 business days.

4

Confirmation Email

You'll receive a refund confirmation email with the transaction reference number once the refund is processed.

03 Booking Modifications

  • 🔄
    Date Changes: You may change your check-in or check-out dates free of charge up to 48 hours before the original check-in date, subject to room availability.
  • 🛏️
    Room Upgrades: Room upgrades are allowed at any time before check-in. The price difference will be charged. Downgrades follow the standard cancellation and re-booking process.
  • 👥
    Guest Name Changes: The name on the booking can be changed up to 24 hours before check-in at no extra cost — once per booking.
  • ✨
    Add-on Amenities: Additional amenities (WiFi, breakfast, parking, etc.) can be added at any time before or during your stay at standard rates.

04 Special Circumstances

  • 🏥
    Medical Emergencies: We offer full refunds for cancellations due to medical emergencies with valid documentation (doctor's note or hospital records). Contact us within 24 hours.
  • 🌪️
    Natural Disasters / Force Majeure: In the event of natural disasters, government-imposed travel restrictions, or other force majeure events, full refunds or free rebooking will be offered.
  • ✈️
    Flight Cancellations: If your flight is cancelled by the airline (not a voluntary change), we'll offer free rebooking or a full refund upon submission of the airline's cancellation notice.
  • 🔒
    Non-Refundable Bookings: Some promotional and discounted rates are marked as non-refundable at the time of booking. These bookings cannot be cancelled or refunded, but date modifications may be available.

05 Package & Group Bookings

  • 🎁
    Weekend Getaway Package: Standard cancellation policy applies. Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in.
  • 👑
    Royal Retreat Package: Cancellations must be made at least 72 hours (3 days) before check-in for a full refund due to the premium inclusions.
  • 💎
    Honeymoon Suite Package: Requires 7 days advance notice for free cancellation. A 25% charge applies for cancellations within 3–7 days.
  • 👥
    Group Bookings (5+ rooms): Group reservations require 14 days advance notice for cancellation. Partial cancellations (reducing room count) are allowed up to 7 days before.
