Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

Our standard check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out is 12:00 PM (noon). Early check-in and late check-out are available upon request, subject to availability. Premium package guests enjoy complimentary late check-out until 2:00 PM.
Yes! We offer complimentary airport transfers for guests booking our Royal Retreat and Honeymoon Suite packages. For other bookings, a luxury sedan can be arranged at ₹1,500 per trip. Simply mention your flight details while booking.
Our rooftop infinity pool is open from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily. Lifeguards are on duty during all operating hours. Suite guests have access to a private heated pool available until midnight.
Bookings can be modified or cancelled up to 48 hours before check-in with no charges. Cancellations within 48 hours will incur a one-night room charge. No-shows will be charged the full booking amount.
We welcome small pets (under 10kg) in our pet-friendly Standard and Deluxe rooms at an additional charge of ₹500 per night. Please notify us at the time of booking so we can prepare special amenities for your furry companion!
Absolutely! Our Grand Ballroom seats up to 500 guests for weddings, conferences, and special events. We also have a smaller Ivory Hall for intimate gatherings of up to 80 people. Our events team will help you plan every detail.
